PEMDAS Calculator is a simple calculator with natural language advanced features.

It supports standard arithmetic operations (+, -, *, /, ^) with proper PEMDAS order of operations and parentheses grouping.

One-parameter functions:
abs, acos, asin, atan, ceil, cos, cosh, cot, coth, csc, csch, exp, fact, floor, ln, log, round, sec, sech, sin, sinh, sqrt, tan, tanh

Two-parameter functions:
log, nrt, pow

Built-in constants:
e, pi, ln2, ln10, log2e, log10e, sqrt1_2, sqrt2
